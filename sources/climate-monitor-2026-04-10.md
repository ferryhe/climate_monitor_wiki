Summary: A new MSCI Institute report (April 8, 2026) finds that physical climate risks are increasingly viewed as a systemic financial stability issue, not just an insurance sector concern. **[UNCTAD] Climate Finance Transparency, Accountability and Effectiveness Need Urgent Improvement**
Summary: A UNCTAD statistical report (published April 7, 2026 via China's Economic Daily) finds that most current climate finance is not genuinely additional — much of it represents reclassified existing official development assistance, with only a handful of donor countries meeting the 0.7% GNI ODA baseline.

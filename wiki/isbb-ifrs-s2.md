# ISSB IFRS S2 — Climate Disclosure Standard

> The ISSB's climate-related disclosure standard (IFRS S2) has been amended, with an effective date of January 1, 2027 for large companies.

## Overview

IFRS S2 (Climate-related Disclosures) is the ISSB's flagship standard requiring companies to disclose climate-related risks and opportunities. The standard builds on TCFD recommendations and is being adopted globally.

## Key Facts

- **Effective date:** January 1, 2027 (early adoption permitted)
- **Scope 3 Category 15 (financed emissions):** Applies to financial institutions only; derivatives excluded
- **CO₂e conversion:** Mandatory; activity-based calculation preferred; location-specific emission factors prioritized over industry averages
- **Nature-related Disclosures:** ISSB advancing consultation draft by October 2026 (COP17) — the "BEES" project renamed to "Nature-related Disclosures" (expanded scope beyond biodiversity)
- Compatible with: TNFD, SASB, GRI, ESRS frameworks to reduce duplicative disclosure burden
- Simplified measurement for Scope 3 financed emissions where data is limited (emerging economies)

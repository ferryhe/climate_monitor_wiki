# Parametric Insurance

> Index-triggered insurance products that pay based on objective environmental measurements rather than actual losses — gaining rapid traction as traditional actuarial models fail.

## Overview

Parametric insurance pays out when a predefined trigger (wind speed, earthquake magnitude, temperature threshold) is reached, regardless of actual insured loss. This removes the need for loss adjustment and enables rapid payout. Particularly relevant for climate events where traditional models are failing.

## Key Facts

- **Parametric premiums: +38% growth since 2021** (Sustainable Atlas, 2026)
- **EU protection gap: 58%** — creates massive product opportunity for parametric solutions
- **2026 accelerating shift to parametric products** for SME climate risk coverage (Forbes, Mar 31, 2026)
- **CAS 2026:** Parametric insurance products gaining traction in energy and agriculture sectors
- **ARC (African Risk Capacity):** Launched Africa's first sovereign parametric flood insurance (Madagascar)
- CDD/HDD derivatives already trade on CME — institutional infrastructure exists

# WRI — Energy Communities & Climate Resilience

> The World Resources Institute published a working paper on the Water-Energy-Food Nexus as a framework for community-level climate resilience in Colombia.

## Key Findings

- **Energy Communities** as mechanism for both climate adaptation and mitigation
- **Water-Energy-Food Nexus** framework for Colombia's Energy Communities Strategy
- Links energy security to food and water resilience
- Directly relevant for actuarial models assessing systemic climate risk at community level

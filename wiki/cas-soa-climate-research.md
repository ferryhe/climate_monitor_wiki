# CAS & SOA — Actuarial Climate Research

> The two main US actuarial societies are actively commissioning and publishing research on climate risk quantification.

## CAS (Casualty Actuarial Society)

### Active RFP — Climate Risk Research (March 24, 2026)
- Up to **$75,000 in awards** for research across 5 tracks:
  1. Perils (cat modeling)
  2. Practice integration
  3. Mitigation investment
  4. Insurability
  5. Risk transfer
- **Proposals due May 22, 2026**
- Signals formal review of traditional catastrophe modeling approaches for systematic actuarial integration
- Source: https://www.casact.org/article/request-proposals-developing-actuarial-frameworks-quantify-and-manage-climate-risk

### CAS: Climate-Driven Typhoon Impact Research
- How evolving climate conditions reshape typhoon cat risk and insured losses
- Updated loss modeling frameworks for pricing and reserving in typhoon-exposed markets
- Source: casact.org | April 2026

## SOA (Society of Actuaries)

### ACI in Weather Derivatives Framework (2026)
- Evaluates how Actuaries Climate Index can be applied within weather derivatives pricing
- CDD and HDD derivatives already trade on CME
- Proposes extending ACI methodology to weather derivatives as complementary risk transfer
- Source: https://www.soa.org/globalassets/assets/files/static-pages/research/arch/2026/arch-2026-iss-1-garrido.pdf

### NGFS Climate Scenarios — Actuarial Framework
- Practitioner's guide for applying NGFS climate scenarios to actuarial assumptions

### SOA — UK Climate Financial Risk Forum
- Collaboration on climate risk quantification frameworks

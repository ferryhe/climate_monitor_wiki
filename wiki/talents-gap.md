# Insurance Talent Gap as Strategic Risk

> The growing shortage of actuarial, risk management, and climate analytics expertise has escalated to a strategic-level business risk for insurers.

## Key Findings

- **InsuranceERM (April 14, 2026):** Talent shortage in insurance/reinsurance has escalated to strategic-level business risk
- Firms struggling to recruit and retain staff with actuarial, risk management, and climate analytics expertise
- As climate-related risks grow more complex, the skills gap is impairing insurers' ability to model, price, and manage these risks effectively
- Direct implications for actuarial function resourcing and future pipeline of climate-capable professionals
